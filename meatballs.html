Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

Start by mixing the ground beef, salt and pepper in a bowl and then, with your hands, shape the it into balls about an inch and a half in diameter. Heat a pan and fry the meatballs on medium heat for about 10 minutes or until they get a dark brown color.

Serve with mashed potatoes and gravy.
