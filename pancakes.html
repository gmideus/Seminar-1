Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Instructions

Mix flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl. Add the egg, milk and melted butter and mix until smooth. Pour the batter into a pan and fry on medium heat for a few minutes.

Serve with maple syrup.
